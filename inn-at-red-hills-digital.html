Inn at Red Hills website and email

The purpose of this project was to redesign a website and brand, and align the clientâ€™s digital marketing efforts to those updates.

I created a complementary website and email template for The Inn at Red Hills, a small hotel in Oregon's beautiful wine country. The intention was to create a mobile-first design, and based on user data, help users quickly check room availability and book.

A mobile view of the Inn at Red Hills homepage redesign.

My process

I really loved some of the decorative shapes found around the property, so I toyed with creating vectorized assets from some of those motifs and using them throughout the site. I also took some of the analytic data that had been collected and created a navigation on mobile devices that provided the most popular actions to take.

A desktop view of the Inn at Red Hills homepage.

I built the email templates from the Foundation for Emails framework. This provided a completely reliable and consistent experience regardless of viewport size, device type, email client, and font availability.
